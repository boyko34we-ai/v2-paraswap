FAQ

About ParaSwap

What is ParaSwap?

ParaSwap is a DEX aggregator that routes token swaps across 20+ decentralized exchanges and liquidity sources to find the best possible rate. It splits orders across multiple pools when it results in better execution.

How does ParaSwap find the best rate?

ParaSwap's MultiPath algorithm simultaneously queries all integrated DEX protocols, splits orders across multiple pools when beneficial, and calculates real-time optimal routes to maximize your output and minimize slippage.

What is the PSP token?

PSP is the governance and utility token of ParaSwap. Stakers earn a share of protocol fees, unlock ParaBoost for better rates, and vote on governance proposals. PSP is distributed to active protocol users.

Does ParaSwap charge extra fees?

ParaSwap charges zero additional fees on swaps. You only pay the standard DEX trading fees and Ethereum gas costs. In fact, ParaSwap often saves you money by finding better routes and returning positive slippage.

What chains does ParaSwap support?

ParaSwap operates on Ethereum, Polygon, Arbitrum, Optimism, Base, Avalanche, BNB Chain, Fantom, and zkEVM — with more networks being added continuously as the DeFi ecosystem expands.

What are Limit Orders on ParaSwap?

ParaSwap's Limit Orders let you set a target price for your swap. The order executes automatically when the market price is reached — gaslessly, without you needing to monitor the market or pay for failed transactions.
